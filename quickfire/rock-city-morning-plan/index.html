🎆 Wait, Is It a Holiday?

July 3rd isn't technically a federal holiday (that's the 4th), but Rock City is running their full "Red, White & Wonder" celebration on both July 3 and 4 this year for America's 250th. Live string music, the bald eagle show, extended hours (9am–10:30pm), the whole deal.

Basically July 4th energy without the actual July 4th crowds.

📍 Quick Facts

  • Address: 1400 Patten Rd, Lookout Mountain, GA 30750
