🎆 Wait, Is It a Holiday?

July 3rd isn't technically a federal holiday (that's the 4th), BUT Rock City is treating it like one! They're running their full "Red, White & Wonder" celebration on BOTH July 3 and 4 this year for America's 250th anniversary. So we get the full festive experience today: live string music, the bald eagle show, patriotic vibes, extended hours (9am–10:30pm), the whole thing.

TLDR: it's basically July 4th energy without the actual July 4th crowds. Smart move, team. 🧠
